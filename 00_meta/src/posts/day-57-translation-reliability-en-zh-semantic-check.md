# Day 57: 'Translation Reliability' (EN/ZH Semantic Check)

- **The Pointer:** Verify that English course content translated to Simplified Chinese preserves the original semantic intent — not just literal meaning.
- **The Details:** Direct translation misses educational register. An agent does a back-translation check and flags any sentence where the Chinese concept differs from the English instructional intent.
- **Action:**

```bash
gemini "Original (EN): [paste]. Translation (ZH): [paste]. Back-translate the Chinese. Flag any sentence where the educational meaning shifted. Output a comparison table."
```
