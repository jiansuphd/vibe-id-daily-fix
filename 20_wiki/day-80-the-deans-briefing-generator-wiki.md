# Day 80: The 'Strategic Briefing' Generator — Wiki

## Core Concept

Auto-generate a one-page executive briefing on course development progress from your vault and project logs. Leadership needs a different view of your work than you need. An agent that reads the progress log, distills the KPIs, and outputs a 250-word executive summary bridges the communication layer.
