# Day 84: The 'Agentic Peer Reviewer' (SME Persona) — Wiki

## Core Concept

Instantiate an AI persona trained on a specific SME's published work and have it review your course content as that expert would. A well-prompted SME persona catches discipline-specific errors that a generic AI cannot. The persona reads the SME's papers, then audits your course for content accuracy and disciplinary alignment.
