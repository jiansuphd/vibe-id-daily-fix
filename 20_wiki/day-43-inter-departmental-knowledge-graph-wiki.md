# Day 43: Inter-Departmental Knowledge Graph (Scaling Context) — Wiki

## Core Concept

Map connections between courses, departments, and skills across the institution using an AI-generated knowledge graph. When you operate at scale, context collapse kills quality. A graph lets the AI 'see' cross-departmental dependencies and prevent curriculum redundancy or gaps.
