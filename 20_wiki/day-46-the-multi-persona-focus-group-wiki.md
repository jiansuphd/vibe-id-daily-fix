# Day 46: The 'Multi-Persona Focus Group' (Synthetic Stakeholders) - Wiki

## Core Concept

Simulate a focus group of synthetic stakeholders to stress-test a course design or policy before real review. Run four AI personas simultaneously: a skeptical faculty member, an accessibility advocate, a budget-conscious dean, and an adult learner. Their objections surface gaps before the real meeting.
