# Day 54: The 'Efficiency-Balanced' AI Agent (Logic Balancing) — Wiki

## Core Concept

Design an agentic workflow that front-loads low-cognitive tasks and protects peak-energy windows for high-value creative work. Not all tasks are equal. An energy-aware agent knows that 'editing alt text' is low-cognitive and should be batched into fatigue windows, while 'designing branching scenarios' requires peak energy.
