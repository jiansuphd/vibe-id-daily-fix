# Day 87: The 'Visual Hierarchy' Vision Audit — Wiki

## Core Concept

Submit any course page screenshot to a vision model and receive a visual hierarchy audit with redesign recommendations. Visual hierarchy is invisible to the author but immediately felt by learners. A fresh AI 'eye' identifies where attention is being split, where CTAs are buried, and where whitespace is wasted.
