# Day 94: The 'Multi-Modal' Executive Brief — Wiki

## Core Concept

Generate a multi-modal executive brief — text summary, visual timeline, and KPI dashboard — from your vault in one pipeline. Executives consume information in multiple modalities. A pipeline that reads your progress log and outputs text + Mermaid timeline + metric table covers all three channels simultaneously.
