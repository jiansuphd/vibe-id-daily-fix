# Day 65: The 'Agentic' Onboarding SOP - Wiki

## Core Concept

Generate a complete onboarding SOP for a new instructional designer joining your team - using your vault as the source of truth. Your vault is your institutional knowledge base. An agent can synthesize the 00_meta/ structure, Work Plan, and skill files into a Day-1 onboarding document that any new ID can follow.
