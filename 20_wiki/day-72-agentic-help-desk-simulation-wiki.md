# Day 72: Agentic 'Help Desk' Simulation — Wiki

## Core Concept

Simulate a faculty help desk interaction to pre-build FAQ responses and identify the top 10 support gaps before a course launches. Faculty ask the same questions every semester. An agent that roleplay-simulates 20 common faculty questions against your course design identifies gaps before they become tickets.
