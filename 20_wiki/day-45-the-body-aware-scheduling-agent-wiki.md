# Day 45: The 'Context-Aware' Scheduling Agent (Workflow Resilience) - Wiki

## Core Concept

Feed your project energy cycles into the CLI to optimize your high-focus tasks. Your energy patterns are real productivity variables. An agent that models energy levels and schedules high-cognitive tasks for peak performance windows is an ergonomic best practice.
