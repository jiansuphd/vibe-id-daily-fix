# Day 46: The 'Multi-Persona Focus Group' (Synthetic Stakeholders)

- **The Pointer:** Simulate a focus group of synthetic stakeholders to stress-test a course design or policy before real review.
- **The Details:** Run four AI personas simultaneously: a skeptical faculty member, an accessibility advocate, a budget-conscious dean, and an adult learner. Their objections surface gaps before the real meeting.
- **Action:**

```bash
gemini "You are four reviewers. React to this course design as: (1) skeptical faculty, (2) accessibility advocate, (3) budget dean, (4) adult learner. Give each a 2-sentence critique."
```
